# 2AFC Experiment: 10 Numerosity Range Blocks + 2 RM Range Blocks

## 10 Numerosity Range Blocks
- 5 sector angles: 40°, 60°, 90°, 120°, and 170°
- 2 reference types:
  - Radial reference and tangential probe
  - Tangential reference and radial probe

Each **numerosity range block** contains **160 trials**.

### Trial Structure
1 block = 160 trials = 8 pairs (for example, for the 40° sector angle and reference numerosity of 9):
- Pairs: 
  - 9 vs 5
  - 9 vs 6
  - 9 vs 7
  - 9 vs 8
  - 9 vs 10
  - 9 vs 11
  - 9 vs 12
  - 9 vs 13
- 10 different displays
- 2 repetitions per pair

This results in **20 data points per condition**.

### Numerosity blocks reference and probe

|sector angle|reference|probe|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|40|9|5, 6, 7, 8, 10, 11, 12, 13|
|60|13|9, 10, 11, 12, 14, 15, 16, 17|
|90|18|14, 15, 16, 17，19，20，21，22|
|120|24|20，21，22，23，25, 26，27, 28|
|170|30|26，27，28, 29, 31, 32, 33, 34|

## 2 RM Range Blocks
- 1 radial reference block
- 1 tangential reference block

Each **RM range block** contains **160 trials**.

### Trial Structure for RM Range
1 block = 160 trials = 8 pairs (for example, for the radial reference condition):
- Pairs:
  - R3 vs T3
  - R3 vs T4
  - R4 vs T3
  - R4 vs T4
  - R4 vs T5
  - R5 vs T4
  - R5 vs T5
  - R5 vs T6
- 2 spacings:
  - Small spacing (discs almost touching each other)
  - Larger spacing
- 10 repetitions per pair

This results in **20 data points per condition** for the RM range.

## Experiment Overview
- **Total blocks**: 12
- **Total trials per block**: 160
- **Total trials**: 12 blocks * 160 trials = 1,920 trials

### Estimated Experiment Duration
- Each numerosity block takes approximately **6 minutes**.
- **Total time**: 10 numerosity blocks * 6 minutes = **60 minutes**.
- Adding time for RM blocks, breaks, and slower responses, the total experiment time is estimated to be **1 hour 30 minutes**.
